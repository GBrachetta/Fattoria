Our Philosophy

Thanks to the commitment of the family, a little fragment of the history of Tuscany was saved and made available to all those who want to have a taste of antiquity and modernity at the same time.
All the buildings were restored and turned into apartments maintaining the rustic style of the original hunting lodge which characterises the whole Fattoria di Collemezzano where you can still hear words like: sala carrozze (coach room), piccionaia (pigeon house), frantoio (oil mill room).
Lines of cypresses and hundreds of olive trees were planted together with oleanders and fruit trees. Now the whole vegetation is very mature and it fills the park making it very special.

Facilities

The swimming pool is surrounded by olive trees and magnificent views of the Cecina valley. The sound of the cicadas will soothe you and transport you to an idyllic world.

Besides the individual dining rooms each apartment has, several spaces in the Fattoria invite our guests to share a meal or the wonderful tarts Francesca prepares with fruits from the orchard.

Our playgrounds, football field and tennis court are open all year welcoming our guests, their families and their pets.
Feel welcome for a casual game, a picnic or even some camping for the little ones.
