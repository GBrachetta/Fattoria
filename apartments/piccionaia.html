Independent apartment cut out of an annexe of the main house where, at the time of Leopold II doves were bred. The building has a very peculiar aspect because of the two dove towers with their cells where once doves used to rest. The apartment has a kitchen completely equipped and a double bedroom. It also has an attic with a bunk bed. The patio outside, shaded by green bushes, is a perfect place for your meals and as a children playground.

From €80 per night
