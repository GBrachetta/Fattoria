The Fattoria

Previous Next

The core of the Fattoria di Collemezzano is the historic and ancient hunting lodge of Leopold II, dating back from the 19th century. The main house together with the annexes (the pigeon house, the coaches room, the oil mill and the chapel) were transformed into the present “agriturismo” by the family Russo over more than 30 years of restoration, dedication and care. The apartments into which the original structures have been transformed evoke the atmosphere of the aristocracy that spent their leisure days here. Our guests enjoy the tranquillity of the place and indulge in pleasant time with friends or family, walks along the lanes lined by old cypresses, or enjoy an informal dinner in the dining room of the main house. Nature surrounds the Fattoria with 43 hectares of woods, olives groves, typical mediterranean trees and bushes. It all makes this Agriturismo the perfect place to relax. A garden, a fruit orchard and farm animals complete the character and atmosphere of a place where one can goe back to the time when life’s pace was determined by nature and its laws. Together with the beauty of the place, a guest can find here the special and unique hospitality of Francesca and her team. Their friendly and warm attention make their guests feel at home and cared for. Children and adults are welcome to share a conversation and a laugh like old friends do. Come and visit us. You will enter a special timeless space where modern and ancient meet.

Where we are

Fattoria di Collemezzano lays on a privileged spot in Tuscany. Surrounded by Nature, Art and History, the region enjoys benevolent weather conditions all year round. The options are endless for every taste, from relaxing in our fields to exciting shopping in some of the most traditional and elegant cities in the world.

Art and Culture

From the richest art cities in the world to the small characteristic medieval “borghi”, the Fattoria di Collemezzano is located in the heart of Tuscany. From here you can easily reach Florence, Pisa, Lucca, or Siena in under one hour. In “day trips” you can visit these towns that are among the most famous for Medieval and Renaissence Art.
Some other very famous places even closer to The Fattoria di Collemezzano, are the medieval towns of Volterra, San Gimignano and Castagneto Carducci.

The Sea

The Costa degli Etruschi (Etruscan Coast) is special for its variety: rocks and long sand beaches are there for any kind of taste and activity The Fattoria di Collemezzano is only 4 km from the closest beach, Le Gorette. There is a free zone “do-it-yourself”. But also organized areas where you can rent a deck chair, a beach bed and unbrella or even a gazebo for those who desire even more comfort. There are also numerous restaurants and cafes that offer from a drink to a full meal.
The coast is characterized by extensive pine woods all along the beach. In the pine wood there are trails to walk and to ride a bike and picnic areas where one can find shelter from the early afternoon sun.
For those who do not mind driving a few kms, there are many other places along the Costa degli Etruschi: particularly attractive is the high rocky coast line from Quecianella to Livorno celebrated in some famous movies. Also worth mention are the long sandy beaches of Marina di Donoratico and the beach of San Vincenzo, as well as the beautiful stony beach of Marina di Cecina.
The whole coast currently has a “ Blue Flag” status as this part of the coast is one of the cleanest in the Tirrenian sea.

Thermal Waters

At a short distance from the Fattoria, there are important spas where our guests can enjoy the beneficial and relaxing effects of thermal natural waters such as Tombolo Talasso Resort (25 mins), Calidario (40 mins) and Cerreta (40 mins).

Sports

Many of our guests are particularly attracted by the wonderful possibilities offered in this area in regard to sports and nature activities. Bikers can leave the Fattoria and ride long distances on the many roads on the hills surrounding the area. We will also provide you with a bike, so unless you want your own bike you do not need to bring one from home.
If you are an individual hiker you can find long trails in the woods on the hills but you can also take guided tours with local experts on alternative itineraries.
Two Km from the Fattoria you'll find “Il Giardino Sospeso”, an Adventure Park where you run acrobatic routes of different difficulties on suspended platforms. Fun is guaranteed to all ages!
Our sea is loved by those keen on surfing and sailing. You can practice these sports in complete autonomy or subscribe for individual or group classes in one of the numerous clubs.
