The Fattoria

Previous Next

The core of the Fattoria di Collemezzano is the historic and ancient hunting lodge of Leopold II, dating back from the 19th century. The main house together with the annexes (the pigeon house, the coaches room, the oil mill and the chapel) were transformed into the present “agriturismo” by the family Russo over more than 30 years of restoration, dedication and care. The apartments into which the original structures have been transformed evoke the atmosphere of the aristocracy that spent their leisure days here. Our guests enjoy the tranquillity of the place and indulge in pleasant time with friends or family, walks along the lanes lined by old cypresses, or enjoy an informal dinner in the dining room of the main house. Nature surrounds the Fattoria with 43 hectares of woods, olives groves, typical mediterranean trees and bushes. It all makes this Agriturismo the perfect place to relax. A garden, a fruit orchard and farm animals complete the farm character and atmosphere where one goes back to the time when life’s pace was determined by nature and its laws. Together with the beauty of the place, a guest can find here the special and unique hospitality of Francesca and her team. Their friendly and warm attention make their guests feel at home and cared for. Children and adults are welcome to share a conversation and a laugh like friends do. Come and visit us. You will enter a special timeless space where modern and ancient meet in harmony and your holiday will be a refreshing break from your tiring daily routine.
